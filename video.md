## 💡 Pro Tips for Engagement

1. **Start with the result** — show the 3D network spinning first, then explain
2. **Use text overlays** — many watch without sound
3. **Fast pacing** — cut dead space, keep it moving
4. **Show the "wow" moment** — the cross-modal search is the hook
5. **End with clear CTA** — "Link in bio" + specific action
